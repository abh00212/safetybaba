Occupational Health & Safety general questions & answer.

Definitions realted to the Health Safety & Environment.

  1. Meaning of Health Safety & Environment.

    • Health, Safety and environment (HSE) refers to a branch, or department, within a company that is responsible for the observance and protection of occupational health and Safety rules and regulations along with environmental protection. Health, Safety and environment (HSE) is also often referred to as environmental health and Safety (EHS) or Safety, health and environment (SHE).

  1. What is Safety in workplace?

    • It is a condition which gives you freedom from hazard, risk, accident which may cause injury, damage and loss to material or property damage and even death.

    • Safety is defined as freedom from those condition that can cause injury to persons including death or damage to property or environment.

    • " Safety" can be define as the absence of danger or physical harm to persons, extending in the workplace to things such as equipment, materials and structures.

  1. Meaning of health as per "WHO".

    • The term "Health" can be defined as a state of well-being in both a physiological and psychological sense. In occupational term, it would include not suffering for example from fatigue, stress, or noise included deafness.

    • As per WHO "Health" is a condition of social, mental and social well being and not merely the absence of disease or infirmity.

  1. Importance of Welfare at workplace and Meaning of Welfare as per "ILO".

    • The term "Welfare" relates to the provision of workplace facilities that maintain the basic wellbeing and comfort of the worker such as eating, washing and toilet facility which enable them to fulfill their bodily.

    • According to ILO, labour welfare can be defined as a term, which is understood to include such services, facilities, and amenities as may be established in or in the vicinity of undertakings to enable the persons employed in them to perform their work in healthy, congenial surroundings and to provide them with amenities conducive to good health and high morale.

  1. Meaning of the environment.

    • The term environment has been derived from a French word “Environia” means to surround. It refers to both Abiotic (physical or non-living) and Biotic (living) environment. The word environment means surroundings, in which organisms live. Environment and the organisms are two organised and complex component of nature. Environment controls the life of the organisms including human beings. Human beings interact with the environment more vigorously than other living beings. Ordinarily environment refers to the materials and forces that surround the living organism.

    • Environment can be defined as the surroundings or conditions in which a person, animal, or plant lives or operates. The term “environment” refers to all elements of the physical and biological world, as well as the interactions between them.

  1. Meaning of the environment protection.

    • "Environment protection" may be defined as a measures used to prevent harm to the environment of the world. it prevents harm to air, water, land and natural resources providing protectionto flora, fauna, and human beings and their inter relationships.

    • Environmental protection refers to any activity to maintain or restore the quality of environmental media through preventing the emission of pollutants or reducing the presence of polluting substances in environmental media.

  1. Meaning of Accident in term of Safety.

    • Accident, unexpected event, typically sudden in nature and associated with injury, loss, or harm, With appropriate Safety precautions and awareness of one's actions and environment, many accidents can be avoided or prevented.

    • It is an unexpected or unplanned event which may or may not result in injury or damage or property loss or death.

    • There are two types of accident as mentioned below.

    • Non-reportable accident: A person may receive a minor physical or bodily injury while working as the outcome of accident. After such injury, if he resumes his duties within 48hrs then such accident called as non reportable accident. Many times person may resume his work on the same day after receiving first aid

    • Reportable or lost-time accident: If the injured person remains absent for 48hrs or more due to injury at workplace, the accident is called as a reportable or lost time accident. As per statutory requirement such incidents are to be reported to the goverment authorities in a prescribed form. The injury in such case is called as major or disabling injury.

  1. Meaning of "Injury" in term of Safety.

    • Injury is defined as a harmful condition sustained by the body as a result of an accident.

  1. Meaning of "Hazards" in term of Safety.

    • Hazard is anything, Which having potential to cause harm.

    • Hazard is defined as a harmful condition sustained by the body as a result of an accident.

  1. Meaning of Risk in term of Safety.

    • "Risk" is a likelihood that hazard will cause a specified harm to someone or something

    • "Risk":- In probability of the realization of potential for loss or damage or injury.

  1. Meaning of Incident in term of Safety.

    • "Incident" is any event which causes or could have cause injury, illness, and damage to plant, material or the environment while accident is an incident which causes damages or injury that is above human capacity

  1. What do you mean by Scaffold in term of Safety?

    • "Scaffold" is a temporary structure/working platform used to support people and material in the construction or repair of buildings and other large structures. It is usually a modular system of metal pipes or tubes, although it can be from other materials. Bamboo is frequently used in Asia.

  1. Work Method statement in term of Safety.

    • "Work method statement" is a document showing the various method through which a particular work is accomplished(Finish). It is also provides a section for the Safety measures for those activities.

  1. What is the use of Work method statement in term of Safety?

    • "Work method statement " describes the method in which a particular job is to be done this can give complete awareness(knowledge) to the working crew. A JSA can be prepared based on the WMS.It helps to finish the job safely.

  1. What is Job Safety analysis JSA in term of Safety & it's use?

    • "Job Safety analysis JSA " is a part of many existing accident prevention programs. In general JSA breaks a job into basic steps and identifies the hazards associated with each step.It also prescribes controls for each hazard.The JSA has to be reviewed durring the investigation if it has been conducted for the job involved in an accident. If one is not available, it has to be performed as part of the investigation to determine the events and conditions that lead to the accident.

  1. What is Permit to work system in term of Safety?

    • "Permit to work " is a formal written document used to control known work place hazards.

  1. What is Confined space in term of Safety?

    • "Confined space " Any are not normally occupied by personnel, having limitedmeans of access and egress and which could be subject to the hazards of oxygen deficiency or the accumilation of toxic or flammable gases or dusts.

  1. What is circumstances a a Confined space work permit can be issued?

    • Gas test is done and the result is favourable.
    • Experienced working crew.
    • Trained entry wath with the entry log sheet.
    • Effective emergency evacuation plan.

  1. Who is a Confined space watchman?

    • The trained person who stands outside the entrance of the confined space with the entry log sheet and records the details of the entrants. He always keeps communication with the entrants. In an emergency, he will call for assistance.

  1. Dangerous Occurence

    • A Dangerous occurrence is any unplanned occurrence which normally results in some loss or damage to machinery and/or the workplace but has not resulted in injury.

  1. What is Near miss in term of Safety?

    • A Near miss is an incident in which no injury or damage results.

  1. What is Work related ill health in term of Safety?

    • A Near miss is an incident in which no injury or damage results.

  1. What is the requirements for Crane lifting Safely?

    • Documents including third party certificates of the crane, Certified rigger, Inspected rigging tackles, barricading the swing radius, lifting permit, lifting plan, JSA, Proper PPE for the crew and favourable climate.

  1. What is the Classes of fire & Fire extinguisher for them?

    • There are following class of fire.

Class of fire and it's extinguisher media.
Class of Fire. Characteristics of Fire. Type of extinguisher or Extinguisher media.
Class of A Fire. Fire involving ordinary organic, solid combustiles such as wood, paper. Water, Wet chemical, Foam spray, ABC powder.
Class of B Fire. Fire involving flammable liquids and liquefiable solids such as petrol. Carbon dioxide, Foam spray, ABC powder.
Class of C Fire. Fire involving fllamable gases such as methane, propane. Gas fires should not be extinguished; unburned gases may cause of explosion, Turn off the gas supply.
Class of D Fire. Fire involving metals such as magnesium, alluminium, etc. Special extinguisher media are required depending upon the metal involved.
Class of F Fire. Fire involving Cooking fat and oil in large catering. Wet chemicals.
