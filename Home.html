NEBOSH

  • The National Examination Board in Occupational Safety and Health is a UK-based examination board offering qualifications and courses in health, safety, environment and well-being management.
  • For more information visit the official websisite of NEBOSH. Click here

IOSH

  • IOSH is the chartered professional body for health & safety in the workplace. It acts as a champion, supporter, adviser, advocate and trainer for those who protect the safety, health and wellbeing of others.
  • For more information visit the official websisite of IOSH. Click here

ADIS

  • ADIS- Advance diploma in industrial safety & PDIS- Post graduate in industrial safety.

HIRA & RA

  • Hazard Identification Risk Assessment is a process of defining hazards by characterizing their probability and severity and evaluating adverse consequences & Damage.
  • A risk is the chance that any hazard will actually cause somebody harm.

Checklist

  • A checklist is a type of job aid used to reduce failure by compensating for potential limits of human memory and attention. It helps to ensure consistency and completeness in carrying out a task. A basic example is the "to do list".
